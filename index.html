Börek

Börek or burek are a family of pastries.The pastry is made of a thin flaky dough such as filo with a variety of fillings, such as meat, cheese, spinach or potatoes.A borek may be prepared in a large pan and cut into portions after baking, or as individual pastries.

Fur coat

Herring under a fur coat is a salad made of herring fillet, vegetables, eggs and mayonnaise, popular in the countries of the former USSR and Israel. The salad got its name due to the fact that the finely chopped herring fillet, placed on a layer of potatoes, is gradually covered with layers of chopped onions, eggs, carrots and beets. Each layer is thoroughly smeared with mayonnaise. Garnish with parsley, green onions, carrot rings, and sprinkle with chopped hard yolk.

Deruny

Deruny - potato pancakes, are very popular in Ukrainian, Polish, Belarusian, Czech, Slovak, Bulgarian, Austrian and German cuisines. The main ingredient of the dish is grated potatoes.
